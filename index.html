About


I am a Senior Mechanical Engineer at ASM, responsible for yield and process improvement, system development and integration, continuous improvement project design, and customer-driven enhancements in Epitaxy Preclean Process Modules.

I earned my Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, in 2024.  My doctoral research specialized in Optical and Optomechanical Systems, focusing on designing and integrating miniature optical imaging systems for early cancer detection.  My work involved imaging system design and integration, optical assembly development, and experimental process validation under the advisement of Dr. Thomas Wang.
