About


I am a Senior Mechanical Engineer at ASM, specializing in yield and process improvement, system development and integration, and the design of continuous improvement projects focused on customer-driven enhancements in Epitaxy Preclean Process Modules.

I earned my Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, in 2024. My doctoral research focused on Optical and Optomechanical Systems with an emphasis on designing and integrating miniature optical imaging systems for early cancer detection. My work included imaging system design, optical assembly development, and experimental process validation under the mentorship of Dr. Thomas Wang.
